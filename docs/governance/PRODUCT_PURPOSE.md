# Product Purpose

## Purpose statement
MindSphere Temporal RAG exists to reduce industrial asset failure risk by providing
fast, explainable, and auditable reliability recommendations grounded in temporal
operational evidence.

## Intended business users
- Reliability engineers
- Turbine operations teams
- Maintenance planners
- Plant performance leadership

## Primary outcomes
- Earlier anomaly-to-action conversion
- Reduced diagnosis time during excursions
- Better alignment of operational controls with historical failure evidence
- Governance-ready AI adoption in regulated industrial settings
